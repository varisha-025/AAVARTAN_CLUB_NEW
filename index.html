EVENTS

INVICTA

Jhankaar was the trademark event for Aavartan during Invicta '20. It was a group dance competition with prizes worth 30,000. The event was judged by a renowned belly dancer. The Aavartan Dance Crew had worked hard for months and ended up giving a performance that completely blew everyone's mind. They proved that blood, sweat, and tears will make anything and everything possible. The year ended with this sweet victory.

FOOT LOOSE

Footloose was an intra-institute dance competition, which was a wonderful opportunity for all the dancers in the institute to show off their talent and win cash prizes! Besides some filler performances by 2nd and 3rd years, there were in all 16 registered performances put up on the stage by the 1st years and the judges were wary about the incoming problem to rank them to get the best 3. All the students were provided with mentors who guided and polished their work throughout the course of a week beginning from finding a suitable choreography to executing it and putting it on stage. A plethora of talent was on display. The sound and light effects added essence to them. At last after 2.5 hours, the following were declared the winners : 1st - Monika More (1811012) Shreeya Patil (1914017) 2nd - Nikhil Barman (2019292) 3rd - Parv Yadav (2019299)

FLASH MOB

Aavartan's trademark event, the promotional flashmob, was held on 18th Jan, where over 50 students had performed a synchronized choreography. This flashmob was done as a part of the promotions for Invicta '20. The students performed twice at the South Avenue Mall. There was a lot of media coverage, and the dancing mob managed to attract many crowds. It is safe to say that the event was a huge success and will go down in history.
